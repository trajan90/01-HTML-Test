Jason Statham is an English actor and film producer known for his roles in action films.
He was born on July 26, 1967, in Shirebrook, Derbyshire, England. Before his acting career,
Statham was a professional diver and also worked as a model. He made his acting debut in the
1998 film "Lock, Stock and Two Smoking Barrels" and has since appeared in many popular films,
including "The Transporter," "Crank," "The Expendables," and "Fast and Furious" franchise.
Statham is known for performing his own stunts and for his physical fitness, having trained in various
martial arts throughout his life. He has received numerous award nominations for his acting,
including a Screen Actors Guild Award for his performance in "The Bank Job."
